ABOUT US

We are a group of people with decades of experience in growing and selling mangoes. We know what a good mango looks like and how they are grown. This essential fruit requires a temperate climate, loamy soil that is rich in organic matter apart from needing proper drainage and aeration facilities. Lucky for us we live in karnataka which is proudly known as paradise of earth is also home to temperate fruits like mango for which the state is very famous across globe. The main factor which influence temperate fruit bearing trees is soil, climate and environment which are highly favorable and unparalleled in the province of karnataka. karnataka mangoes a unique look, taste, flavor, size and color.

VARIETIES

Irwin

This is a smooth, sweet, and flavoursome mango, which is virtually fibreless.

Alphonso

It is the best mango in the world. The fruit is prized for its rich flavour,sweetness, and juiciness.

Ataulfo

Ataulfo mango is a small, bright yellow mango, named for its honey sweet flavour.

Carabao

Carabao mango is the sweetest mango in the world.

Amrapali

The Amrapali mango is revered for its rich and aromatic taste,as its sweetness takes center stage.The fruit is small, and sigmoid shaped.

Sensation

The sensation mango is a late season mango cultivar that originated in south Florida and was grown on a commercial scale.
